Badrinath Singhal

Hi, thanks for dropping by! Outside of work, I try not to take things too seriously. I enjoy cricket, running, guitar, comedy, food, tea, sunrises/sunsets, and mathematics.

Bio: Previously I worked at Axera Tech and EmbodyMe as a Machine Learning Scientist. I graduated from IIT Guwahati with a major in Electronics and Electrical Engineering and a minor in Computer Science. I attended Kendriya Vidyalaya C.R.P.F. Amerigog for secondary school.

I enjoy sharing this talk by Richard Hamming on doing great research (the transcript is especially good). Also here is Neil Patrick Harris hosting Tony Awards 2013 that I like to watch.

Feel free to reach out for discussions, collaborations, or coffee!
